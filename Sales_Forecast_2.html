Phase 2b. Total Sales Characteristics¶

We create histograms in order learn about the company's sales by store and by department.

We learn these:

  1. Store 33 has the least total sales (\$33.7 million). Store 33 is type A and has size 39,690.
  2. Store 22 has the most total sales (\$274 million). Store 22 is type B and has size 119,557.
  3. The four departments with the least total sales across all stores are:
    • Dept 47 with sales of \$-4654.25 (negative sales)
    • Dept 43 with sales of \$14.32
    • Dept 39 with sales of \$40.90
    • Dept 78 with sales of \$1711.71
  4. The three departments with the most total sales are:
    • Dept 92 with sales of \$437.5 billion
    • Dept 95 with sales of \$407.5 billion
    • Dept 38 with sales of \$358.3 billion
