Weekly Sales Forecast for Big Box, Inc.¶

Big Box, Inc. requested a forecast of weekly sales for the individual deparments in each of its stores. The company provided the data team with three CSV files containing data related to its stores and their departments, weekly sales, and economic and weather conditions in the geographic areas where stores are located. Specifically Big Box requested a forecast of the next three months beyond the actual sales data provided. We performed a time series analysis through four phases:

Time Series Analysis Phases¶

  1. Data Import, Validation, and Merge
  2. Exploratory Data Analysis: Stores and Departments
  3. Exploratory Data Analysis: Weekly Sales
  4. Feature creation and selection
  5. Model Training and Testing
  6. Forecast Weekly Sales

Phase 2. Exploratory Data Analysis: Stores and Departments¶

Type and Sizes of Stores¶

We created histograms in order learn about the type and sizes of the company's stores.

We learned these characteristics:

  • Most of the stores are Type A and Type B. Fewer stores are Type C.
  • The sizes of stores vary greatly. The smallest group and the largest group, together, make up half of the stores.
